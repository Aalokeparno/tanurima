“In her painter’s journey of just more than a decade Tanurima was always a rolling stone. She galloped from feminine figure to portraits to the world of insects to the rural scenario. Her masterly grip over her dots and dashes, miniscule those might be called, never betrayed her. Now she has arrived at a destination where she can freely play with her imagination as well as with that of her viewers. We can hear the ultimate ‘Voice without a Form’. Here Tanurima is restless, adventurous, sensuous yet soft as she always is.”

~ Debanjan Sengupta
